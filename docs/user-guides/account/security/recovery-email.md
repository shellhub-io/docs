# Recovery Email

If you have an account on ShellHub [Cloud](https://cloud.shellhub.io), the
recovery email is your last resort to regain access to your account. While not
required at registration, once provided, you cannot reset it to a blank email.
We do not send any verification email to this address, but we assume it is a
valid address. Additionally, the same email address can be used by multiple
users.

When requesting access recovery through this method, ShellHub will send two
emails containing unique codes: one to your **main email** and another to your
**recovery email**. You must provide both codes to regain access, and each code
is valid for 1 day.

:::note for enterprise edition end users
You won't be able to recover your access by yourself with a recovery email. You
need to contact the administrator of the server instance.
:::
